# Preface

Welcome to **Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics: Agent-Native Future**, a comprehensive textbook for engineers, researchers, and AI enthusiasts.

This book explores:

- The integration of ROS 2 with digital twins
- Humanoid movement and control systems
- AI agent integration and multi-agent intelligence
- Memory, perception, reinforcement learning, and human–robot interaction
- Hands-on projects and capstone challenges

**Learning Approach:**
- Beginner → Intermediate → Advanced modules
- Real-world projects with simulation and hardware
- Step-by-step integration of AI agents into humanoid robots.
